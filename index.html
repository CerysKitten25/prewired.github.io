Prewired.

An Edinburgh programming club for under 19s

Less than 19 years old...

..and interested in learning to code? Already know how and want to go even further?

Web, mobile, games... software, hardware... designing, coding, making... anything goes.

Computers let you create anything you can think of. Prewired is here to help enthusiastic young coders of any ability to do just that.

To find out what the deal is, join the mailing list or facebook group, drop us an email or just come along to one of our meetings. Everyone is welcome!

Want to help out, mentor, hold a workshop or sponsor us? We'd love to hear from you - please get in touch below.
