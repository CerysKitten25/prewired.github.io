Prewired.

An Edinburgh programming club for under 19s

We're always looking for more helpers!

Helping young people learn to code is hugely rewarding. If you can offer your time for two hours at 5.30pm on the 1st and 3rd Wednesdays of every month, please email hello[at]prewired.org to let us know. (Please get in touch before showing up to a session).

Mentoring

Prewired is largely unstructured and our main goal is to support the young people in learning whatever it is they want to learn. So mentoring at Prewired consists mostly of wandering around a room full of budding hackers, answering questions, helping to fix bugs, and making sure they're happy and learning.

Any and all software and hardware skills are welcome! At present (March 2014) we have young people working with:

  • Python basics
  • Python games
  • Front-end web development
  • Scratch
  • Minecraft mods
  • C++
  • Java
  • Lego Mindstorms
  • Raspberry Pi
  • Arduinos

We don't necessarily always have mentors to cover all of these, so if you know anything about these topics... and anything else... please get in touch!

Workshops

From time to time we hold a more structured workshop about a specific topic, and encourage all of the young people to get involved and learn something they might not otherwise have thought about. If you'd like to run a workshop we'd love to have you - just get in touch.

Organising

If you're interested in getting more involved in the organising side of things, let us know that too!
