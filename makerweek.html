Prewired.

An Edinburgh programming club for under 19s

Prewired every day: 7th - 12th April

The Prewired Maker Week is a chance for everyone to start and finish a whole project.

This is a great chance to practice some of the things you've been learning, solve a real problem, or just make something really cool.

We also have a couple of extra special trips. Read on for more information...

Sign up

1. Register on the Eventbrite for the whole week.

2. Let us know here which times you'll be there. This will also help your friends know when you'll be there, so you can team up easily.

Monday - Thursdsay: Making

There will be mentors in the computer lab (same place as normal) every day between 10am and 2pm. here are some ideas of what to do each day:

Monday: Come up with ideas of things to make. Sketch them out, figure out what you need to do. Make teams so you can share the work if you like

Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday: Work on your project! Don't forget to ask for help if you need it.

Friday: Science Festival

A special trip to the Madlab electronics workshop at the Science Festival. You need to sign up for that separately here.

Even if you can't make it to the rest of the Maker Week, you can still come to the Science Festival with us!

Saturday: Prizes!

Finish your project, and spend five minutes showing it off and telling everyone about what you've made. Win prizes!

What to bring

Bring your laptop of course. If you have any special equipment (eg. Arduinos, Raspberry Pi etc) that you want to use, bring that too.

We'll have some snacks every day, but you should bring a packed lunch.

You could make...

  • A website about your cat
  • An app that tracks how much time you spend on your homework
  • A Scratch game
  • Pacman in Python
  • A really cool Minecraft mod
  • A map of everybody who is talking about football on Twitter in Edinburgh

...actually, anything you like!

Feel free to completely ignore these suggestions. If you can think of it, and you can make it in one week, there's no excuse not to!

This is made possible thanks to all our mentors, and the support of New Media Scotland's Alt-w Fund.

Mini Maker Faire: Sunday 20th April

Prewired has a table at the Mini Maker Faire!

You're all invited to come along and show off things you've made. This can be what you made during the Maker Week, or any other projects you've been working on.
